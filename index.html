Jamie Gorson is currently a Ph.D student at Northwestern UniversityinTechnology and Social Behavior,, a combined program in Computer Science and Communications. Her interest is in designing and building technology to increase hands-on and project-based learning in classrooms, museums and informal education spaces. She is currently working on Tune Table, a tangible interactive museum exhibit where users can create music by employing computational theories. Jamie received her B.S. from Olin College of Engineering in Electrical and Computer Engineering. Her research is funded by the National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship Program in the field of STEM Ed.
