Current Projects

Tune Table - EarSktech

In congunction with a team from Georgia Tech, we are developing a museum exhibit which uses computational theories to create music. The Tune Table is based on the Georgia Tech Project called EarSketch, a web-app that teaches users to code while creating music.

Past Projects

Brightbox: A Hands-On Learning Technology for Rural India

I worked on a team to develop, build and deploy the Brightbox, a ray projector to aid in teaching kids optics. This technology will be used in schools in rural India and Ghana to encourage exploratory learning and develop a curiosity for science.

Capture It: A Mobile Design App for Kids

For my capstone engineering project, my team and I developed and built Capture It , an application in DS Solidwork's new educational app ecosystem called Apps for Kids. Capture It is a brainstorming application aimed at kids aged eight to fourteen, meant to inspire creativity and curiousity in the design process
